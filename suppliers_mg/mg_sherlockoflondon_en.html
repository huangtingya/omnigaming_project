MG Plus - Sherlock of London

2019 August Written By： Microgaming Photo By：Microgaming

Criminal Master Minds are occupying London once again and the Great Britain Metropolis needs the help of Sherlock Holmes and his faithful sidekick, Dr Watson.

Feature

1.5X3 Reels Slot

2.Medium Volatility

3.25 Paylines

4.Connecting Wilds

5.Free Spins

Available on: Desktop HTML5 & H5 Mobile & Viper

The famous detective can solve any case and that is why he is the main protagonist of Sherlock of London, a five-reel, 25-payline video slot that is packed with additional features. Help Sherlock discover his enemies and he will reward you with Connecting Wilds, Double Wins, Bonus Spins and the Free Spins feature with Extra Wilds and Expanding Wilds. Visually impressive, the Rabcat title is playable from £0.25 per single spin.

Sherlock Homes is the Wild that can substitute for all regular symbols and will also trigger certain features that are active both during the base game and the Free Spins feature. His old friend, Dr Watson, is among regular symbols. Three Criminals are on the loose, and we believe that one of them is Moriarty. There is also a Female Criminal and a One-eyed Criminal on the reels. Holmes’ Address is also among the regular symbols, as well as four different playing cards. Holmes and Watson are together on the Scatter that triggers the Free Spins feature.

Features:

Thanks to the Connecting Wilds feature, if two or more Wilds appear on the reels, all Wilds will be connected and all symbols that are on the connecting path will be turned into Wilds. Once you land two Wilds in the same row on connecting reels, you will activate the Double Win feature and double all wins from that spin. If you land one Wild on reels 1 or 2, you will get 2 Bonus spins, with the Wild locked on its position, while other reels respin. If the Bonus spins result with at least one more Wild on the reels, you will activate either the Connecting Wilds feature or the Double Win feature.

Land three or more Scatters to trigger the Free Spins feature and get 10 freebies. Also, Dr Watson will turn into an Extra Wild during the feature, but cannot trigger the Double Win feature, Connecting Wilds feature, or the Expanding Wild feature, which is available only during freebies. If you land a Holmes Wild on the reels during free spins (only one Wild), it will expand and cover the entire reel.
